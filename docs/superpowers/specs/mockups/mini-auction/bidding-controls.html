Bidding Controls Breakdown

Stepper (+/-): Each tap adjusts by $1M. Hold for rapid increment.
Quick pills: +$1M (active/highlighted), +$2M, +$5M, +$10M — jump fast when you want to make a statement bid.
Fund info: "Available" and "After bid" update live as you adjust the stepper.
BID button: Shows the exact amount — one tap to confirm. No extra confirmation dialog (speed matters).
Minimum: Stepper can't go below current bid + $1M. Max capped at your budget minus reserved slots.
